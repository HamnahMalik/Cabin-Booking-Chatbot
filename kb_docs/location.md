# Pine Ridge Cabins — Location (Demo)

- Address: 88 Lakeview Road, Cedar Falls (Demo)
- Nearest airport: Cedar Falls Regional (CFR) ~ 35 minutes by car
- Nearest grocery: Pine Market ~ 12 minutes
- Nearest hospital: Cedar Falls Medical Center ~ 20 minutes

## Directions (from downtown Cedar Falls)
1. Take Route 6 North for ~10 miles
2. Turn right onto Lakeview Road
3. Entrance is on the left after ~1.2 miles
