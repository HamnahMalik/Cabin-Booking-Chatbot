# Pine Ridge Cabins — Policies (Demo)

## Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in: 3:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Check-out: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in: subject to availability (fee may apply)
- Late check-out: request in advance (not guaranteed)

## Cancellation Policy
- Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival.
- 6–2 days before arrival: 50% refund of nightly rate.
- Within 48 hours of arrival or no-show: no refund.

## House Rules
- Quiet hours: 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM
- No smoking inside cabins (smoking allowed outside)
- Maximum occupancy must be respected

## Pets
- Pet-friendly cabins available (dogs only).
- Fee: $25 per night per dog (max 2 dogs).
- Pets must be leashed outside.

## Parking
- One free parking space per cabin
- Extra vehicles: $10/night (subject to space)
