# Pine Ridge Cabins — Cabin Types (Demo)

## Studio Cabin (2 guests)
- 1 queen bed, kitchenette, private deck
- Great for couples
- Some units are pet-friendly

## Family Cabin (4 guests)
- 1 queen bed + 2 twin beds
- Kitchenette and dining nook
- Best for small families

## Lakeview Deluxe (2 guests)
- 1 king bed, premium lake view deck
- Optional hot tub add-on (limited units)
- Not pet-friendly (demo rule)
